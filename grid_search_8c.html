Performs the CMT depth grid search.

Parameters
[in]l1number of sites
[in]ndepsnumber of depths
[in]nlatsnumber of latitudes (northings)
[in]nlonsnumber of longitudes (eastings)
[in]verbosecontrols the verbosity (0 is quiet)
[in]deviatoricif true then constrain the inversion s.t. resulting moment tensor is purely deviatoric. otherwise, invert for all six moment tensor terms.
[in]utmSrcEastingssource easting UTM position (m). the ilat,ilon'th position in the grid is given by ilon*nlats + ilat [nlats*nlons]
[in]utmSrcNorthingssource northing UTM position (m). the ilat,ilon'th position in the grid is given by ilon*nlats + ilat [nlats*nlons]
[in]srcDepthssource depths (km) in grid-search [ndeps]
[in]utmRecvEastingreceiver easting UTM positions (m) [l1]
[in]utmRecvNorthingreceiver northing UMT positions (m) [l1]
[in]staAltstation elevations above sea level (m) [l1]
[in]nObsOffsetobserved offset in north component (m) for i'th site [l1]
[in]eObsOffsetobserved offset in east component (m) for i'th site [l1]
[in]uObsOffsetobserved offset in vertical comonent (m) for i'th site [l1]
[in]nWtsweight corresponding to i'th north offset observation [l1]
[in]eWtsweight corresponding to i'th east offset observation [l1]
[in]uWtsweight corresponding to i'th vertical offset observation [l1]
[out]nEstestimate offset in the north component (m) for the i'th site at all depths, lats, and lons. [l1*ndeps*nlats*nlons] the i'th site at the idep'th depth is given by idep*l1 + i
[out]eEstestimate offset in the east component (m) for the i'th site at all depths, lats, and lons. [l1*ndeps*nlats*nlons] the i'th site at the idep'th depth is given by ilon*nlats*ndeps*l1 + ilat*ndeps*l1 + idep*l1 + i
[out]uEstestimate offset in the vertical component (m) for the i'th site at all depths [l1*ndeps*nlats*nlons] the i'th site at the idep'th depth is given by ilon*nlats*ndeps*l1 + ilat*ndeps*l1 + idep*l1 + i
[out]mtsthe moment tensor terms (Nm) inverted for in an NED system at each depth. the id'th depth is begins at index 6*id. the moment tensors at at each depth are packed: . [6*l1*ndeps*nlats*nlons]
Returns
0 indicates success
