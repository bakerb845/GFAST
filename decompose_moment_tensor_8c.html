Finds the moment tensor decomposition of the NED moment tensor.

Parameters
[in]nmtnumber of moment tensors to decompose
[in]Mmoment tensors (Nm) in NED for all nmt moment tensors. the i'th moment tensor is begins at index 6*i. the moment tensor packing is: [6*nmt]
[out]DC_pctdouble couple percentage for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]Mwmoment magnitude (e.g. Kanamori 1977) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]strike1strike of nodal plane 1 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]strike2strike of nodal plane 2 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]dip1dip of nodal plane 1 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]dip2dip of nodal plane 2 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]rake1rake on nodal plane 1 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
[out]rake2rake on nodal plane 2 (degrees) for each moment tensor [nmt]
Returns
0 indicates success
