Meshes a fault plane from the magnitude, strike, and dip computed in the CMT inversion. Note the fault patch at (istr, idep) is accessed by kndx = idip*nstr + istr. For more information on the magnitude to fault size scaling see D. Dreger and A Kaverina, Seismic Remote Sending for the Earthquake Source Process and Near-Source STrong Shaking: A Case Study of the October 16, 1999 Hector Mine Earthquake. Geophysical Research Letters, Volume 27 (No 13), 1941-1944, (2000).
